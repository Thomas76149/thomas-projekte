What it's about

Up, and up, and up.

A simple idea taken to the extreme: a roller coaster that just keeps climbing. Hop in the cart, feel the physics, and watch the ground disappear beneath you.

Climb to the sky

The whole point is height. Ride the track up and up until everything below turns tiny.

Real physics

The cart actually rolls, builds speed and reacts — it's the ride that makes it fun, not a script on rails.

Sky-high views

From the top you get a view you can't get anywhere else — the kind that makes you screenshot it.

Easy to hop into

No setup, no learning curve. Get in the cart and enjoy the ride — alone or with whoever's around.
